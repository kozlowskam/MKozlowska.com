'010' A PHOTOGRAPHIC COMPANION TO THE ALBUM.

Photobook is featuring archival images from the vaults of the National Archive of the Netherlands. The images are individually curated by each artist. This photobook serves as a supplement and extra layer of context to the 010 compilation. With these images, the music, their inspiration and history can come to life and turn this whole body of work in a cohesive soundtrack to the great city of Rotterdam. Designed by Malgorzata Kozlowska for RE:VIVE, an Initiative by the Netherlands Institute for Sound and Vision.

'010' A photographic companion to the album. With this compilation RE:VIVE team up with Fog Mountain Records, to bring 14 tracks that take archival sounds from the Sound and Vision collection and turn them into soundtrack of Rotterdam city.
'010' album is available for free download via Fog Mountain Records.
